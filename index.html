DATE · Privacy Policy · 隐私政策

Effective 2026-07-24 · Updated 2026-07-24

DATE 记录 不收集、不上传、不存储用户的任何个人数据。

我们收集哪些数据？

不收集任何数据。DATE 记录 是一款完全本地运行的 iOS 应用。你在 App 中创建的所有内容——包括倒数日的标题、日期、备注——仅保存在你自己的设备上（使用 iOS 系统提供的 SwiftData 本地数据库），不会被上传到任何服务器。

我们如何使用你的数据？

由于不收集数据,因此不存在"使用"问题。你的数据完全由你自己控制,我们（开发者）无法访问、无法读取、无法修改。

数据共享

DATE 记录 不与任何第三方共享你的任何信息。开发者本人也无法接触到你 App 里的内容。

联网访问

DATE 记录 不联网。App 从不主动发起任何网络请求,不加载任何远程内容、图片或脚本,不上报任何使用统计。

第三方 SDK

DATE 记录 不集成任何第三方 SDK,包括但不限于:分析统计、广告投放、崩溃上报、用户追踪、社交登录等。

Cookie 与追踪

由于不联网,DATE 记录 不使用任何 Cookie 或追踪技术。

儿童隐私

DATE 记录 面向所有年龄段用户（App Store 分级 4+）,不针对 13 岁以下儿童收集或处理任何数据。由于本 App 完全不收集数据,因此也不适用于任何专门针对儿童的隐私法规（如美国 COPPA）。

数据删除

你的所有数据仅存储在你的设备本地。删除 DATE 记录 App 即彻底删除所有数据,无需向开发者提出删除请求（因为开发者本来就没有你的数据）。

你的权利

由于本 App 完全不采集数据,因此不涉及数据主体权利的行使。你对 App 内的所有数据拥有完全的控制权。

政策变更

如果本隐私政策发生变更,我们将在本页面上公布最新版本,并更新页面顶部的"更新日期"。建议你不定期回访本页面查看最新版本。

DATE does not collect, upload, or store any personal data from users.

What data do we collect?

None. DATE is a fully local iOS app. Everything you create in the app — countdown titles, dates, notes — is stored only on your device (using Apple's local SwiftData database) and never uploaded to any server.

How do we use your data?

Since we don't collect any data, there is no "use" to speak of. Your data is entirely under your control. We (the developer) cannot access, read, or modify it.

Data sharing

DATE does not share any of your information with any third parties. The developer has no way to reach any content stored within your DATE app.

Network access

DATE does not connect to the internet. The app never makes network requests, never loads remote content or images, and never reports usage analytics.

Third-party SDKs

DATE does not integrate any third-party SDKs, including but not limited to: analytics, advertising, crash reporting, user tracking, or social login.

Cookies and tracking

Since DATE does not connect to the internet, no cookies or tracking technologies are used.

Children's privacy

DATE is intended for users of all ages (App Store rating 4+). We do not collect or process any data from children under 13. Because the app collects zero data, it does not fall under child-specific privacy regulations such as COPPA (U.S.).

Data deletion

All your data lives only on your device. Deleting the DATE app deletes all your data permanently. No deletion request needs to be sent to the developer (because we never had your data to begin with).

Your rights

Since no data is collected, there are no data-subject rights to exercise via the developer. You have complete control of all data within the app.

Policy changes

If this Privacy Policy changes, we will publish the updated version on this page and revise the "Updated" date at the top. You're encouraged to check this page occasionally for the latest version.
